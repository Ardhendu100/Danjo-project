Approval by AICTE

RCM has been approved by All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE). The Institute follows the AICTE guidelines and course structure. AICTE has provided an extension of approval for the academic year 2020-18.

Accredited by NAAC

Regional College of Management is accredited by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) of University Grants Commission (UGC), since 2010 for its quality and innovative education.

Approved by UGC

RCM and the Degrees awarded by it, are recognized by University Grant Commission (UGC). The college has been approved under section 2(f) and 12 (b) of the UGC Act in the year 200

RCM has been accredited by the National Board of Accreditation of AICTE since 2003.

RCM is an ISO: 9001-2008 quality certified educational institution.
